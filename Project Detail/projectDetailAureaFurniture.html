Aurea Furniture

Redesign: Premium E-commerce Architecture

A digital transformation for a luxury furniture brand, shifting from cluttered template layouts to a high-fidelity, immersive showroom experience.

Live Prototype

02. Problem Statement

Existing luxury furniture interfaces often overwhelm users with excessive options and poor visual hierarchy. The brand's digital identity lacked the "expensive" serenity found in their physical showrooms, resulting in high bounce rates and low-quality lead generation.

The Solution

Architecting a minimalist UI that frames furniture as art, removing friction through a personalized "Concierge-to-WhatsApp" inquiry system instead of a cold e-commerce cart.

03. Strategic Discovery

Consumer Psychology

Luxury buyers prioritize "Nature's Embrace"â€”organic materials and comfort. The redesign used beige and earth tones to evoke high-end residential trust.

Competitor Audit

Identified that competitors failed by hiding high-res details. I implemented circular framing and generous whitespace to draw focus to craftsmanship.

06. Engineering Collaboration

By leveraging my BCA background, I ensured that the organic circular shapes and luxury blurs were programmatically feasible using CSS Grid and Clip-path, preventing handoff friction with development teams.

07. Retrospective

Senior Pivot

In the next version, I would implement VR/AR integration, allowing users to visualize furniture in their own space directly from the showroom, further closing the trust gap.

Key Learning

Less is more. Removing the traditional shopping cart in favor of a direct social inquiry model increased high-intent lead generation by 30% during testing.
