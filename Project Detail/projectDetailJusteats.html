02. Problem vs Solution

The Problem

Local pizza shops are losing 30% revenue to aggregator commissions, stripping them of the ability to provide free hyper-local delivery and maintaining direct customer relationships.

The Solution

A high-fidelity digital interface designed for direct ordering. This removes the middleman, restores shop margins, and uses immersive UI to build trust between the Chef and the customer.

03. Strategic Discovery

Hyper-Local Logistics

Optimized the user flow for in-house delivery staff. By limiting the digital radius, shops can offer "Free Delivery" to their immediate neighbors, a key competitive advantage.

Human-Centric UX

Integrated Chef profiles to replicate the "local shop" feel. Using Figma's high-end prototyping tools, I created a discovery-first journey rather than a generic utility app.

04. Execution Artefacts

Full Interactive Journey

The transition from menu discovery to secure payment. Every screen uses a consistent Atomic Design system to maintain brand integrity.

"About Us" & Chef Ritual

To bridge the trust gap, the 'About Us' section focuses on the kitchen's history and the Chef's expertise, creating a personal connection that delivery aggregators lack.

05. Engineering Feasibility

Utilizing my BCA background, I ensured that the high-fidelity mockups remained programmatically feasible. Every visual element—from the shadows to the grid spacing—was delivered with ready-to-code Figma tokens.
