02. Problem Statement

Commercial drivers in India waste an average of 40 minutes daily searching for authorized parking. Existing apps are cluttered, lacking EV infrastructure tracking and real-time timer visibility, which leads to heavy late-fines and safety risks during high-traffic intervals.

Business Objective

Design a 3-click booking system with an integrated countdown timer and EV-charging status, optimized for safe, thumb-friendly operation for cab, car, and bike operators.

03. User Flow Mapping

Designed for high-stress urban environments. The flow ensures that from "Intent to Payment," the user never performs more than 3 distinct actions.

04. Wireframe Origins

Thumb-Friendly Design: Every CTA and navigation trigger is placed within the comfort zone of a commercial driver's grip. These lo-fi wireframes validate the visibility of the "Time Remaining" countdown to prevent late penalties.
