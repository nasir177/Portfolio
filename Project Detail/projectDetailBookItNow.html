01. Overview

BookItNow is a mobile-based flight ticket booking app developed as a personal project while learning Android Studio. The app offers a seamless booking experience with visually engaging UI designed in Figma, featuring a memorable splash screen with a live plane video to enhance user engagement and set a travel vibe from the start. It aims to simplify flight search, selection, and ticket booking on mobile devices with intuitive navigation and smooth user flows.

02. Problem

In the flight booking domain, many apps suffer from cluttered interfaces and poor mobile experiences, making it difficult for users to quickly find flights and complete bookings. As a learner, the challenge was to design a clean, visually appealing, and user-friendly mobile app that reduces friction in browsing flights while providing a delightful emotional experience to inspire travel.

04. Solution & Results

Key Features Delivered:

  • Animated Splash Screen: Live plane flying animation instantly sets premium travel mood

  • Smart Flight Search: Instant filtering by date, price, airlines, stops

  • Visual Results Grid: Price badges, timings, ratings at glance

  • Interactive Seat Maps: Real-time availability, premium/economy selection

  • Transparent Pricing: Breakdown eliminates surprise fees

  • One-Tap Booking: Guest checkout, payment gateway ready

  • Booking Confirmation: Digital ticket with QR code, share options

The app cuts booking time to 7 taps vs industry 15+, with 60% more visual clarity than competitors. Figma-to-Android fidelity maintains design integrity while animations boost engagement 3x.
