Parkkaro

A 2D Japanese Neobrutalist approach to turning idle urban spaces into passive income.

P2P Business Model
Real-time Firebase Sync
Problem Statement

The High Cost of Idle Space

Driver Anxiety

Urban drivers spend an average of 15 minutes searching for spots, increasing traffic congestion and stress levels.

Homeowner Loss

Homeowners have empty driveways and idle plots that sit unproductive. There is no bridge between seekers and owners.

UI Flow: The Driver Journey

Splash & Neobrutalist Auth

A high-fidelity video intro sets the 2D aesthetic tone. The Auth gate uses Neobrutalist high-contrast borders to ensure maximum legibility for users on the go.

Business Edge: Branding consistency starts at T-minus 0 seconds.

Real-Time Discovery

Powered by Geoapify, the dashboard provides a "Driving-First" UI. High-contrast slot markers ensure users can find nearby spots with a single glance while operating a vehicle.

UX Logic: Reducing cognitive load via color-coded occupancy.

Booking & Secure QR

Instant price calculation based on duration. The flow ends in a unique QR code, acting as a digital "Key" for the host’s gate scanner.

Strategic Evolution

Car.Park V1 to Parkkaro V2

V1: The Prototype

A foundational tool focused on simple discovery and basic booking logic. It solved "Where can I park?"

V2: The Passive Income Ecosystem

Introduces peer-to-peer hosting. Any homeowner can now list their space. It solves "How can I earn?"

The Identity Switch

Inside the Profile, a high-contrast toggle flips the identity from "Driver" to "Host," triggering an atomic Navbar swap and database pivot.

UI Flow: The Host Journey

Analytical Dashboard

Real-time business tracking using Firebase listeners. Hosts see "Active Parkers" and "Daily Revenue" live, transforming their space into a managed asset.

Business Value: Immediate feedback on asset performance.

3-Step Space Hosting

A multi-step experience designed to remove "Business Fear." Homeowners list spots by providing a Nickname, Pincode, Image, and Amenities (EV/CCTV) in under 60 seconds.

The Gatekeeper Scanner

A specialized QR scanner allows hosts to verify vehicles, ensuring site security and automating the checkout process for precise revenue calculation.

The Parkkaro Solution

Parkkaro democratizes urban space. By integrating Geoapify for precision and Firebase for real-time logic, we have turned a daily liability—parking anxiety—into a shared asset class for urban dwellers.

This ecosystem doesn't just find slots; it provides a comprehensive passive income ritual for space owners while maintaining the speed and safety requirements of active drivers.
