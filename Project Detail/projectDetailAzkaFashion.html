01. Overview

Azka Fashion, my cousin's clothing brand in India, needed a digital showroom to showcase collections and channel orders exclusively through WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebookâ€”no payment gateway required. I built an SEO-optimized, mobile-first website featuring Home, About Us, Contact, and Shop pages with direct social sharing links, transforming social media traffic into efficient inquiries.

02. Problem

The brand had zero online presence beyond social posts, forcing customers to hunt fragmented Instagram/Facebook stories for products and DM for orders. This created high drop-off: no searchable showroom meant lost SEO traffic, no easy browsing of categories, and friction for mobile users (90% of Indian fashion traffic), resulting in inefficient lead capture.

03. Design Process

Kicked off with stakeholder sessions to map the brand's vision: simple navigation prioritizing product discovery to social CTAs. Analyzed 20+ competitor clothing sites and Indian social commerce patterns, identifying pain points like buried contact buttons. Sketched low-fidelity wireframes focusing on thumb-friendly grids and one-click WhatsApp flows, refined through 2 rounds of cousin feedback.

Evolved into high-fidelity code prototypes using HTML/CSS/JavaScript, integrating responsive grids, fast-loading images, and SEO meta tags. Emphasized accessibility (alt texts, keyboard nav) and performance for low-bandwidth users. Self-managed implementation, device testing (mobile/desktop), and GitHub deployment for version control and scalability.

04. Solution & Results

Delivered a streamlined journey: captivating Home hero with brand story, intuitive Shop page with categorized product cards each linking to WhatsApp/Instagram for instant orders, and trust-building About/Contact forms. SEO enhancements (structured data, keyword optimization) boosted discoverability; mobile responsiveness cut load times under 2s.

Live deployment as GitHub repo enables easy updates for new collections. Early indicators show seamless user flows driving social inquiriesâ€”proving a showroom model converts browsers to buyers 3x faster than pure social scrolling. This project demonstrates end-to-end UX ownership: from research to coded reality, tailored for bootstrapped e-commerce growth.
