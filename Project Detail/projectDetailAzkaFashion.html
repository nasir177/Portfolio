02. Problem Statement

Azka Fashion faced a critical discovery gap. By relying exclusively on Instagram stories and DMs, product information was ephemeral and fragmented. Users lacked a central, searchable digital showroom, leading to high friction in the buying journey and lost SEO opportunities.

Business Objective

Engineer a high-performance digital showroom to centralize discovery and drive friction-less conversion funnels from social curiosity to direct WhatsApp inquiries.

The Evolution: V1 to V2

V1: Freelance Production

Built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Focused on core digital presence, semantic SEO, and rapid load times.

V2: Advanced Product Vision

Implemented a modular Design System and high-fidelity smart-prototyping to optimize user journeys.

03. Strategic Discovery

User Research

Interviews with high-intent buyers revealed that "anonymous" social media storefronts lacked credibility. Users demanded a professional catalog to validate product quality before engaging.

Persona Mapping

Identified the "Active Browser" persona: users who discover via social but convert through direct, trust-based communication channels like WhatsApp.

Artefacts: Competitor Audit & Consumer Psychology Analysis

04. Technical Execution

User Journey & IA

Mapped the browse-to-order flow to ensure zero-friction navigation. The new Information Architecture reduced the path to inquiry by three interactive steps.

Low-Fidelity Prototyping

Rapid lo-fi wireframes allowed for testing mobile grid layouts and "thumb-friendly" navigation patterns before final skinning.

05. Scalable Design System

Constructed a robust system in Figma using Atomic Design principles. Focused on reuse and systemic consistency for future scaling.

06. Engineering Collaboration

Utilizing my BCA background, I ensured the V2 vision was technically feasible. I optimized V1 for Core Web Vitals and SEO, ensuring a robust handoff between aesthetic design and performant code.

07. Retrospective

Challenge & Solution

Handling unoptimized high-res fashion assets was a major performance bottleneck. Implementing a lazy-loading strategy and WebP format ensured a load time under 1.2 seconds.
