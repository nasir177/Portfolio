The Problem Statement

Discovery Gap & Credibility Loss

User Research

Interviews revealed that "anonymous" social storefronts lacked credibility. Users demanded a professional catalog to validate quality before bargaining.

Business Goal

Switch from fragmented Instagram DMs to a centralized, searchable showroom that converts social curiosity into direct sales via WhatsApp.

Information Architecture

Designed a zero-friction path from discovery to inquiry. The flow specifically optimizes for a D2C model where sales occur through personal communication rather than a cart.

Logic: Social-to-WhatsApp Flow

Wireframe Origins

Lo-fi wireframes focused on mobile "thumb-friendly" grid layouts. Mid-fi testing validated that users preferred a high-quality "Brand Story" approach over generic shopping lists.

High-Fidelity Vision

Implemented a modular Design System in Figma. Focused on high-contrast visual cues that guide the user to the "Inquire via WhatsApp" primary call to action.

Evolution: V1 to Azka Brand Design
